About Ace Academy

Ace Academy is an online school in collaboration with the Telangana Government Organization. It provides a strong foundation for future success through its online education programs.

We have experienced and qualified tutors who mentor each student according to their individual capabilities. Our 1:1 teacher-student ratio ensures personalized attention and minimizes distractions.

We follow the British Curriculum for students up to Nursery from to Intermediate 2 year, we use the books published by the State Council of Educational Research and Training (SCERT) of Telangana. Telugu is a compulsory subject, with an option to choose between Urdu and Hindi.

Our curriculum is designed to enhance students' growth by prioritizing and creating more opportunities for learning. Ace Academy is more than just an online school; it's a partner in your child's educational journey. Together with our experienced tutors, supportive community,

and comprehensive curriculum, we aim to unlock your child's full potential and set them on a path to success.
